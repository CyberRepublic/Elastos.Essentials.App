About red packets

Traditionally used in China to celebrate special occasions and share happiness among friends on social networks, red packets are derived from this idea in Elastos Essentials to celebrate, but also to create crypto communities entertainment.

Red packets introduce much more fun compared to “airdrops”, as luck and reactivity are involved.
